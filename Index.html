GIGACHAD

"Gigachad" was an online persona given to the model Ernest Khalimov after doing a photoshoot for a project by Krista Sudmalis called "Sleek'N'Tears" but only became popular from a redditor that posted his Instagram page on r/bodybuilding. Claiming he was "Gigachad, a perfect human specimen destined to lead us against the reptilians"

The rise of Gigachad

On February 7th,2021, content creator, Socialism Done, posted a meme in the "Average Fan VS. Average Enjoyer" format that gained major popularity on Youtube, Twitter, and Tik Tok, with many more versions posted by other people.Gigachad was gaining millions of veiws all over the internet and yet Khalimov did not speak about being used as the "Gigachad" memes. Before his meme popularity grew, Khalimov had little online activity, which, combined with his unwillingness to acknowledge his online popularity, was interpretted on the internat as fitting the overall "Gigachad persona"
